# php-musicplayer
<p>PHP simple music player.</p>
<br />
<p>Built by me and Gemini.</p>
<p>The files are in Hungarian, but you can change it as you like.</p>
<p>If PHP is not yet installed on your web server: <a href="https://www.php.net/downloads.php" target="_blank">https://www.php.net/downloads.php</a></p>
<br />
<p>index.php --></p>
<ul>
  <li>lists the mp3 files available in the directory</li>
  <li>reads the metadata of the files (artist, title, album, album art)</li>
  <li>minimalist, simple ui</li>
  <li>option to search for title or artist</li>
  <li>option to switch the order of the list by title or album</li>
  <li>option to add songs to queue</li>
  <li>press the "SPACE" button or click the icon to play/pause</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + "arrow-right" button or click the icon to skip forward</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + "arrow-right" button or click the icon to skip backward</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + "arrow-up" button or adjust the slider to turn up the volume</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + "arrow-down" button or adjust the slider to turn down the volume</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + 'M' button or click the speaker icon to mute/unmute the audio</li>
  <li>press the CTRL/CMD + 'S' button or click the icon to shuffle/unshuffle the list</li>
  <li>press the "TAB" button to switch focus between the search and the list</li>
  <li>press the "ESC" button to defocus either the search or the list (while in focus)</li>
  <li>press the "ESC" button to go up a directory (while not in focus)</li>
  <li>press the "BACKSPACE" button to go to the previous page (while not in search)</li>
</ul>
